The Original Taste of Jizzakh in Tashkent

Since 1995, Islom Bobo Choyxonasi has stood as a beacon of authentic Uzbek culinary tradition. We didn't just open a restaurant; we built a local landmark dedicated to perfecting one thing: the legendary Qipchoq Somsa.

Baked in traditional wood-fired tandoors, our recipes have been passed down through generations. No shortcuts, no modern compromises. Just pure, handmade authenticity that our community has trusted for decades.
